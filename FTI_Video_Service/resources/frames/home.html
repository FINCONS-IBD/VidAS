The FTI Video Analysis Service aims at demonstrating some of the supporting features of the FTI Video Service; that is functionalities related to:


- Users' management devoted to the management of customers' data (e.g., FTI, airlines, leasing companies), as well as of their personnel authorized to use the FTI Video Service ,


- Video storage/retrieval devoted to manage video files (for the current prototype these elements will be managed as simple files),


- Video access policies management through which FTI customers, and FTI, will be able to define access policies governing the video files encryption process so that only users having profiles with the set of attributes specified in the policy could actually be able to decrypt the file,


- Access key management through which authorized users can obtain their personal decryption keys


- Users' authentication and authorization.
